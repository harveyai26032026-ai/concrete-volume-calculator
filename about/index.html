About Concrete Calculator

Concrete Calculator is a free, client‑side web tool that instantly estimates concrete volume, bag counts, mix ratios, and saw‑cut spacing. It runs entirely in your browser—no sign‑up, no ads, and no data is sent to our servers.

The calculator supports both metric and imperial units, offers advanced options such as graded‑land calculations for uneven ground, and provides industry‑standard mix‑ratio guidance.

This tool is part of the Nexus Web Tools family, a collection of free utilities for construction, finance, and engineering professionals.
